About Us

At tvunah.ai, we are pioneering the future of quality risk management through artificial intelligence. Our platform revolutionizes how organizations handle quality risks by consolidating disparate sources of risk information into a comprehensive, unified Quality Risk Register (QRR).

We understand that managing quality risks across multiple data sources—deviations, complaints, audits, and observations—can be overwhelming and time-consuming. Our AI-driven solution brings clarity and efficiency to this critical process, enabling organizations to make informed decisions faster and more accurately.

Founded by experts in quality management and artificial intelligence, tvunah.ai is committed to helping organizations maintain the highest standards of quality and compliance while reducing the burden of manual risk assessment and documentation.

Our Technology

Our advanced AI platform leverages cutting-edge machine learning and natural language processing to transform quality risk management.

AI-Powered Analysis

Our sophisticated machine learning algorithms analyze vast amounts of quality data from multiple sources, identifying patterns, trends, and correlations that would be impossible to detect manually.

Natural Language Processing

Advanced NLP technology extracts meaningful insights from unstructured text in deviation reports, complaint descriptions, audit findings, and observation notes, automatically categorizing and prioritizing risks.

Unified Data Integration

Seamlessly integrates data from disparate systems and sources—deviations, complaints, audits, and observations—into a single, coherent Quality Risk Register that provides a comprehensive view of your risk landscape.

Real-Time Risk Assessment

Continuous monitoring and real-time risk scoring ensure that emerging risks are identified and escalated immediately, enabling proactive risk management and faster response times.

Predictive Analytics

Predictive models forecast potential future risks based on historical patterns, helping organizations stay ahead of quality issues before they become critical.

Automated Reporting

Generate comprehensive reports and dashboards automatically, saving countless hours of manual work while ensuring consistency and accuracy in risk documentation.
